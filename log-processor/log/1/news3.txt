Team-up Rule

All full-time employees and contractors may join a team. Partners, vendors, instructors, clients, or outsourcing staff may join if approved by the appropriate CBU.

Each team must have exactly 5 people (no more and no less).

Each participant may join only one team at a time.

Participants in the regional warm-up game may change to completely different teams for the global contest.

A Taiwanese team may include some members from other regions.

You can find AI expert teammates here: refer to "TW A.I. Expert" tab

Find your group by team leader's GJM: refer to "The Groups & Facilitators" tab
